Webgazer

What we do: We are trying to examine the feasibility of doing eye-tracking experiments using consumer grade webcams.

Why we do: Eyetracking experiments are traditionally very expensive and must be done in lab. Therefore, the data collected is limited. We wish to find cheaper and more efficient ways to collect eyetracking data.

What you need to do: You will look at various different objects, such as dots and images, on the screen.

Privacy concern: We do not collect your name, or any sensitive information. All data are stored securely in our database.
